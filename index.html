The goal is never to be indispensable. I build systems so predictable they run without daily intervention, so your team scales without adding overhead.

A workflow your team actually uses beats an elegant one they ignore. I design for adoption first, then optimize based on what's actually being used.

Leaders make better decisions when they see the full picture in one place. I build reporting that surfaces what matters most without adding noise.
